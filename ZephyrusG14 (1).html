2021 Asus ROG Zephyrus G14

As our top pick, this shouldn't come as a surprise which combines performance and portability, Powered by top of the line Processors and blazing fast Nvidia graphics, amazing build quality built with tough materials, it is sturdy yet durable to the touch, 14 inches to its display size for its compact size suitable for gamers and content creators on the go,this machine offers both performance and portability on another level

Premium Build quality

The G14 sports a magnesium chassis and keyboard deck that promises a premium feeling of the laptop. The white coloured finish further expresses that elegant feeling

  • Durable chassis ensures the durability of the laptop for years to come
  • Elevated keyboard for maximum air flow and more comfortable typing for long hours
  • Dual fans for higher sustained performance

Performance and portability combined

Powered by Nvidia RTX graphics and AMD Ryzen, all packed in 1.7Kg of weight

  • Packed with an 8 core 16 thread CPU and Nvidia graphics, configurable from GTX 1650 up to RTX 3060, gamers and content creators will be pleased with its performance on the go with its light weight build.
  • Can be configured with dual channel RAM for more performance gains
  • With Asus' "Armoury Crate" software, Users are able to configure their machine to offer more performance or battery life

Excellent port selection

Various ports for peripherals

  • 180w Barrel pin charging
  • 2 USB A 3.2 gen 2 ports
  • 1 HDMI 2.0b for external display
  • 1 USB C for charging and peripherals

There are some cons worthy of noting though.

Cons to pay attention to:
  • Fans can be quite loud on high loads
  • There is no webcam
  • The warm heat output goes to the bottom of the display, making it quite hot when gaming
