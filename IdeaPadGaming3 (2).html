IdeaPad Gaming 3

As our top choice for the top budget gaming laptop we have the IdeaPad Gaming 3, from Lenovo. It comes at a reasonable price without compromising much in gaming performance, its no wonder why students and casual gamers would choose this laptop.


Features


Run your favourite games smoothly.

Comes equipped with an AMD Ryzen™ 4000H-series processor, NVIDIA® GeForce® GTX Graphics, DDR4 memory, and m.2 NVMe PCIe SSD storage options. These modern internals will allow you to run your games at high settings so that you can see every detail and get the best graphics to boost gaming experience.



High refresh rate display.

The IdeaPad Gaming 3 gives you a blue carpet in your match history with a display that accommodates low input latency. It comes with a 15.6” FHD (1920 x 1080) IPS display with FreeSync that goes up to 120Hz so that you can take down multiple targets in-game effortlessly and pile up killstreaks like a professional gamer as you should be.



Top of the line keyboard.

The IdeaPad Gaming 3's full sized keyboard was designed for gamers, with 1.5mm key-travel for ultimate control, designed to have crisp tactile feedback to characterize each keystroke, and blue backlighting to help in individual key visibility in dark conditions.



Long lasting gaming hours.

Enjoy the privilege of gaming anywhere you want with a long lasting 45 Wh battery pack that fully recharges in an instant with Lenovo's Rapid Charge Technology. One hour of charging gives you up to 80% battery life which is equivalent to 3 hours of harsh gaming.



Specs


  • AMD Ryzen™ 5 4800H (6 cores/12 threads) mobile processor up to AMD Ryzen™ 7 4800H (8 cores/12 threads) mobile processor.
  • NVIDIA® GeForce® GTX 1650 4GB up to NVIDIA® GeForce® GTX 1650ti 4GB.
  • RAM 8GB 3200MHz up to 32GB 3200MHz of DDR4 memory. Two SO-DIMM slots capable of dual channel.
  • 15.6” FHD (1920 x 1080) IPS, 60Hz, 45% NTSC, 250 nits, up to 15.6” FHD (1920 x 1080) IPS, 120Hz, 45% NTSC, 250 nits.
  • M.2 PCIe SSD up to 1TB or 2.5” SATA HDD 5400 RPM 1TB or both. Maximize storage to store all your games.
  • 45Wh battery pack that can last up to 6.7 hours, with Rapid Charge from Lenovo.
  • Comes in Onyx Black and Chameleon Blue
  • I/O, 2 x USB-A 3.1 Gen 1, USB-C 3.1, HDMI 2.0, RJ45 Ethernet port, 3.5mm Headphone / Mic combo jack.
